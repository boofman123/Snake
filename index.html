Extreme Snake!

How to Play:
  • Use the arrow keys, WASD, or the buttons below to move the snake.
  • Munch the tasty food to grow and score points!
  • Collision with the yourself, the walls or large objects will result in death!
  • As you increase the score, speed increases
  • You have three lives for smaller objects!
  • Try to get the highest score possible!
